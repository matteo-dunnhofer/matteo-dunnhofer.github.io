Marie Skłodowska-Curie Postdoctoral Fellow

Lassonde Building, York University, 4700 Keele St, Toronto ON, M3J 1P3 Canada

matteo.dunnhofer@uniud.it matteo24@yorku.ca

I am currently a Marie Skłodowska-Curie Postdoctoral Fellow (employed as RTT). I work between the Department of Mathematics, Computer Science, and Physics of the University of Udine (Udine, Italy), and the Centre for Vision Research at York University (Toronto, Canada).

I come from Tarvisio, a small town located in the Italian Alps. I received the BSc and MSc in Computer Science and the PhD in Industrial and Information Engineering from the University of Udine in 2016, 2018, and 2022 respectively. I have been a visiting student at the Australian Centre for Robotic Vision of the Queensland University of Technology (Brisbane, Australia). More recently, I spent time as a visiting researcher at the Massachusetts Institute of Technology (Cambridge, USA), and at the University of Alcalà (Madrid, Spain).

My research interests revolve around deep learning for computer vision, in particular I like to develop visual object tracking algorithms.
